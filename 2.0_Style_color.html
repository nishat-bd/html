Bangladesh

Bangladesh, located in South Asia, is a vibrant and densely populated country known for its rich cultural heritage and stunning natural beauty. It shares its borders with India to the west, north, and east, while Myanmar (Burma) lies to the southeast.

The country is characterized by its lush green landscapes,the intricate network of rivers, and the world's largest river delta, the Sundarbans, home to the elusive Bengal tiger. Dhaka, the capital, is a bustling metropolis, and the country's people are known for their warmth and resilience. Bangladesh has a diverse cultural tapestry, with a strong influence of Bengali traditions, a rich history of art and literature, and a passion for cricket.
